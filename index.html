About me

Hello there My name is Biruk Tesfaye. I am a Second year IT
student at Addis Ababa institute of technology. I was born in
Addis Ababa, capital city of Ethiopia. I finished high school
at Glory primary and secondary school. Everyone told me that
campus life is very hard and unbearable. They are not wrong
, life in here is very hard especially when there are too many
projects and assignments to do. But that is why I came here for, to
be educated and get something out of it. My plan is to be an ethical hacker.

Ethical hacker is a cyber security expert whose job is to try and break in to the
company network and access their personal data to ensure it is locked carefully away
behind as many levels of security as necessary.

I am also interested in Photshop and 2D animation. I started learning
photoshop when the pandemic begun. Since then I have been trying to
master Adobe photoshop. I also started learning 2D animation on blender
but it is too complex and since class has started I had to pause it. Am looking
forward to becoming the best graphic designer.
